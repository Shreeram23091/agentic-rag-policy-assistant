ACME CORPORATION — EMPLOYEE LEAVE POLICY
Document Version: 3.1 | Effective Date: January 1, 2024
=========================================================

1. ANNUAL LEAVE
---------------
All full-time employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Part-time employees accrue leave on a pro-rata basis proportional to their contracted hours.

Leave can be carried over to the next calendar year, up to a maximum of 5 days.
Any unused leave beyond this limit will be forfeited on January 1 each year.

Employees must submit leave requests at least 5 business days in advance via the HR portal.
Emergency leave requests may be submitted with less notice at the manager's discretion.

2. SICK LEAVE
-------------
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
Sick leave does not carry over; unused sick days are reset on January 1.

For absences exceeding 3 consecutive days, a medical certificate from a registered physician is required.
Sick leave cannot be used in conjunction with annual leave without manager approval.

3. MATERNITY & PATERNITY LEAVE
--------------------------------
Primary caregivers (maternity): 26 weeks of leave — first 16 weeks at full pay, remaining 10 weeks at 50% pay.
Secondary caregivers (paternity): 4 weeks of fully paid paternity leave.
Adoption leave mirrors maternity/paternity entitlements.

Employees must notify HR at least 8 weeks before the expected commencement date.

4. UNPAID LEAVE
---------------
Employees may apply for unpaid leave of up to 12 weeks per year for personal reasons.
Approval is at the sole discretion of the department head and HR.
Unpaid leave does not affect annual leave accrual.

5. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
------------------
Employees are entitled to all gazetted public holidays. If required to work on a public holiday,
employees will receive double their daily rate or an equivalent compensatory day off.

6. EMERGENCY & COMPASSIONATE LEAVE
-------------------------------------
Up to 5 days of paid compassionate leave is granted for the death of an immediate family member
(spouse, child, parent, or sibling). Up to 2 days for extended family members.

Emergency leave (e.g., natural disasters, accidents) is assessed case-by-case by HR.

7. STUDY LEAVE
--------------
Employees enrolled in company-approved professional development programs may apply for up to 3 paid
study days per year for examination preparation or attendance.

8. CONTACT
----------
For all leave-related queries, contact HR at hr@acmecorp.com or ext. 2200.

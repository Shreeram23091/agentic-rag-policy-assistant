ACME SAAS PLATFORM — PRODUCT FAQ
Version: Q1 2024 | Support: support@acmecorp.com
=================================================

GENERAL QUESTIONS
-----------------

Q: What is the ACME SaaS Platform?
A: The ACME SaaS Platform is a cloud-based project management and team collaboration tool that helps
   organizations plan, track, and deliver projects efficiently. It integrates with over 50 third-party
   applications including Slack, Jira, GitHub, and Google Workspace.

Q: What pricing plans are available?
A: We offer three plans:
   - Starter: Free, up to 5 users, 3 active projects, 1 GB storage
   - Professional: $12/user/month, unlimited projects, 50 GB storage, priority support
   - Enterprise: Custom pricing, SSO, SLA guarantees, dedicated account manager, unlimited storage

Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes. The Professional plan offers a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. You can upgrade or
   downgrade at any time from your account settings.

ACCOUNT & BILLING
-----------------

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email, and follow the link sent to
   your inbox. The reset link expires after 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check spam or
   contact support@acmecorp.com.

Q: Can I change my billing cycle from monthly to annual?
A: Yes. Navigate to Settings > Billing > Change Plan. Switching to annual billing gives a 20% discount.
   The change takes effect at your next billing cycle.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. Enterprise customers may request invoiced
   billing with NET-30 payment terms.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the billing
   period. Data is retained for 30 days post-cancellation, after which it is permanently deleted.

FEATURES & INTEGRATIONS
------------------------

Q: Does the platform support Gantt charts?
A: Yes. Gantt charts are available on Professional and Enterprise plans. Navigate to any project and
   switch to the "Timeline" view. You can set dependencies, milestones, and critical paths.

Q: Can I integrate with Slack?
A: Yes. Go to Settings > Integrations > Slack and authorize the connection. You can configure
   notifications for task assignments, comments, and due date reminders.

Q: Is there an API available?
A: Yes. A REST API is available for Professional and Enterprise users. Full API documentation is at
   developers.acmecorp.com. Rate limits: 1,000 requests/hour for Professional, 10,000 for Enterprise.

Q: Does the platform support Single Sign-On (SSO)?
A: SSO via SAML 2.0 and OAuth 2.0 is available on the Enterprise plan. Supported identity providers
   include Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, and OneLogin.

DATA & SECURITY
---------------

Q: Where is my data stored?
A: Data is stored in Microsoft Azure data centers in the US East region by default. EU customers can
   opt for EU West (Ireland) to comply with GDPR. Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).

Q: Is the platform GDPR compliant?
A: Yes. We are fully GDPR compliant. We act as a data processor; your organization is the data controller.
   A Data Processing Agreement (DPA) is available upon request. Contact legal@acmecorp.com.

Q: What is the uptime SLA?
A: Starter: No SLA. Professional: 99.9% uptime (less than 8.7 hours downtime/year).
   Enterprise: 99.99% uptime (less than 52 minutes downtime/year). Status page: status.acmecorp.com.

ACME CORPORATION — REMOTE WORK POLICY
Document Version: 1.8 | Effective Date: February 15, 2024
==========================================================

1. ELIGIBILITY
--------------
Remote work (full-time or hybrid) is available to permanent employees who have completed at least
6 months of employment and whose role does not require a full-time physical presence.
Eligibility is subject to manager approval and department needs.
Employees on performance improvement plans (PIPs) are not eligible for remote work arrangements.

2. HYBRID WORK SCHEDULE
------------------------
The default hybrid model requires a minimum of 3 days per week in the office.
Core in-office days are Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday.
Managers may adjust in-office requirements based on team needs, with at least 2 weeks' notice.
Fully remote arrangements (0 days in-office) require VP-level approval and annual review.

3. HOME WORKSPACE REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------
Employees working remotely must maintain a dedicated, professional workspace with:
- A reliable internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps download / 10 Mbps upload recommended)
- A private space suitable for confidential calls and video meetings
- Adequate lighting and ergonomic setup

The company will provide a one-time home office stipend of $500 for approved remote employees.
Receipts must be submitted within 90 days of approval. Approved items: desk, chair, monitor, keyboard, mouse.

4. EQUIPMENT
------------
Company-issued equipment (laptop, headset) is provided for remote work.
Employees are responsible for the safekeeping of all company equipment at home.
Lost or damaged equipment due to negligence may be charged to the employee.
Personal devices may be used only if enrolled in MDM (per IT Security Policy).

5. WORKING HOURS & AVAILABILITY
---------------------------------
Remote employees must be available and responsive during core hours: 10 AM – 4 PM local time.
Attendance at scheduled meetings is mandatory regardless of location.
Employees must update their status on Microsoft Teams during working hours.
Overtime while remote follows the same authorization process as in-office overtime.

6. PERFORMANCE & ACCOUNTABILITY
---------------------------------
Performance expectations are identical for remote and in-office employees.
Managers will conduct monthly check-ins (minimum) with remote employees.
Output and deliverable quality will be the primary measure of performance, not hours logged.

7. SECURITY OBLIGATIONS
-----------------------
Remote employees must adhere fully to the IT Security Policy (see separate document).
Company VPN must be active when accessing any internal systems.
Confidential information must never be discussed in public spaces.
Screens must be locked when leaving the workstation.

8. EXPENSES
-----------
Internet and phone expenses are not reimbursable for standard remote work.
Travel to the office on required in-office days is not reimbursable unless the employee lives more than
50 miles from the office, in which case up to $150/month in travel costs may be claimed.

9. TERMINATION OF REMOTE ARRANGEMENT
--------------------------------------
The company reserves the right to revoke remote work privileges with 2 weeks' notice if:
- Performance declines below expectations
- Team collaboration is negatively impacted
- Business requirements change

Employees may voluntarily return to full in-office work at any time by notifying HR.

10. CONTACT
-----------
Remote work agreements must be filed with HR: hr@acmecorp.com | ext. 2200

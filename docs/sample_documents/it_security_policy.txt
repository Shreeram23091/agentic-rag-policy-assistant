ACME CORPORATION — IT SECURITY POLICY
Document Version: 2.4 | Effective Date: March 1, 2024
======================================================

1. PASSWORD POLICY
------------------
All employee passwords must meet the following requirements:
- Minimum 12 characters in length
- Must contain at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one digit, and one special character
- Passwords must be changed every 90 days
- Reuse of the last 10 passwords is prohibited
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all systems accessing company data

Password managers (e.g., 1Password, Bitwarden) are recommended and company-licensed tools are provided.
Never share your password with anyone, including IT support staff.

2. DEVICE & ENDPOINT SECURITY
------------------------------
All company-issued devices must have:
- Full-disk encryption enabled (BitLocker for Windows, FileVault for macOS)
- Antivirus software installed and updated (CrowdStrike Falcon is the standard)
- Operating system and software patches applied within 72 hours of release

Personal devices used for work (BYOD) must be enrolled in the Mobile Device Management (MDM) system.
Connecting unapproved USB storage devices to company systems is strictly prohibited.

3. DATA CLASSIFICATION
-----------------------
Data is classified into four tiers:
  - PUBLIC: Information intended for external audiences (marketing material, public docs)
  - INTERNAL: General business information for employees only
  - CONFIDENTIAL: Sensitive business data — requires encryption at rest and in transit
  - RESTRICTED: Highly sensitive (PII, financial records, IP) — access is role-based and logged

Employees must not store CONFIDENTIAL or RESTRICTED data on personal devices or unapproved cloud services.
Approved cloud storage: Microsoft OneDrive, SharePoint. Unapproved: personal Google Drive, Dropbox.

4. EMAIL & PHISHING
--------------------
Do not open attachments or click links in unsolicited emails.
Report suspicious emails to security@acmecorp.com immediately.
The IT team will NEVER ask for your password via email or phone.
Phishing simulations are conducted quarterly; failures require mandatory training.

5. REMOTE ACCESS & VPN
-----------------------
Remote access to internal systems is only permitted via the company-approved VPN (Cisco AnyConnect).
Public Wi-Fi must never be used without an active VPN connection.
VPN credentials are personal and must not be shared.

6. INCIDENT REPORTING
----------------------
All security incidents (suspected breaches, lost devices, unauthorized access) must be reported
within 1 hour of discovery to the IT Security team at security@acmecorp.com or the incident hotline: ext. 9911.
Failure to report known security incidents is a disciplinary offense.

7. ACCEPTABLE USE
-----------------
Company systems are for business use. Limited personal use is permitted provided it does not:
- Consume excessive bandwidth
- Access inappropriate or illegal content
- Compromise system security

Social media access is permitted during breaks. Sharing confidential company information on social media is prohibited.

8. VIOLATIONS
-------------
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination,
and may be referred to law enforcement where applicable.

Contact the IT Security team: security@acmecorp.com | Hotline: ext. 9911

STM32 Product Overview

STM32 is a family of 32-bit microcontrollers developed by STMicroelectronics.

Key STM32 series include:
- STM32F4: High-performance microcontrollers
- STM32L4: Ultra-low-power devices
- STM32H7: Advanced high-performance processors
- STM32G0: Cost-efficient general-purpose MCUs

Applications:
- Industrial automation
- IoT devices
- Automotive electronics
- Consumer electronics
- Smart home systems

Development Tools:
- STM32CubeIDE
- STM32CubeMX
- ST-Link Debugger

Programming languages commonly used:
- C
- C++
- Embedded C

STM32 devices support interfaces such as UART, SPI, I2C, CAN, USB, and Ethernet.

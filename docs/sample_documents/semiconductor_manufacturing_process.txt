Semiconductor Manufacturing Process

STMicroelectronics follows advanced semiconductor fabrication processes.

Major stages:
1. Wafer fabrication
2. Oxidation
3. Photolithography
4. Etching
5. Ion implantation
6. Metallization
7. Packaging
8. Testing

Cleanroom protocols are mandatory.

Employees must:
- Wear ESD-safe equipment
- Follow contamination prevention rules
- Use antistatic wristbands
- Maintain environmental compliance

Quality checks are performed during each production stage.

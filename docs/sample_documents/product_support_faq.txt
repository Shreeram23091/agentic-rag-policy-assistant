Product Support FAQ

Q: Which IDE is recommended for STM32 development?
A: STM32CubeIDE is the official recommended IDE.

Q: Which debugger is commonly used?
A: ST-Link debugger.

Q: Which operating systems are supported?
A: Windows, Linux, and macOS.

Q: Which programming language is mainly used?
A: Embedded C.

Q: What communication protocols are supported?
A: UART, SPI, I2C, CAN, USB, and Ethernet.
